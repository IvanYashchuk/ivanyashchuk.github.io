I attended FEniCS'17!

Jun 18, 2017 00:00 · 103 words · 1 minute read

This week I was at the FEniCS Conference at University of Luxembourg. I got a chance to meet my mentors personally and other members of the FEniCS community as well.

There were a lot of interesting people and a lot of interesting talks from a diverse range of scientific areas: Software and Algorithms, Magnetics, Optimisation, Biomechanics, Visualisation and Large Scale Computing, Solid and Fluid Mechanics.

Thanks to my mentors Chris, Jack and Martin for inviting me there!
