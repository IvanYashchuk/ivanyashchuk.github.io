Aug 17, 2017 00:00 · 113 words · 1 minute read

As the result of my work during summer, it is now possible to solve PDEs on quadrilateral and hexahedral mesh. Assembly of finite element forms works for CG finite element space. From the user side all that is needed to do is just changing the mesh to one consisting of quadrilaterals instead of triangles or hexahedrons instead of tetrahedrons.

Unit square domain and unit cube domain can be created as following:

quadrilateral_mesh = UnitQuadMesh.create(n, m)

hexahedral_mesh = UnitHexMesh.create(n, m , k)
